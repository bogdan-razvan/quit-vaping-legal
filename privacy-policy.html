Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Summary: We don't collect any data. All your data stays on your device. Nothing ever leaves your device.

1. Introduction

QuitVaping ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any data. QuitVaping is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. Your quit journey is entirely private.

3. Data Storage

All data you enter into QuitVaping (such as your quit date, vaping habits, and progress) is stored exclusively on your device. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

If you delete the app, all your data is permanently deleted from your device.

4. What This Means For You

  • Your quit journey data stays 100% on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No account creation required
  • No personal information collected
  • Complete privacy and anonymity

5. Third-Party Services

If you choose to make a purchase, payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app
  • Your data is never accessible to us or anyone else

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
