Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Summary: We collect minimal data to help you quit vaping. We don't sell your data. Your health journey is private.

1. Introduction

QuitVaping ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Quit Date: The date you started your quit journey
  • Usage Habits: Information about your previous vaping habits (pods per day, cost) to calculate savings and progress
  • App Usage: Anonymous analytics about how you use the app to improve our service
  • Subscription Data: Purchase information processed securely through Apple's App Store

3. Information We Don't Collect

  • Your name or personal identification
  • Your email address (unless you contact support)
  • Your location data
  • Health data from Apple HealthKit
  • Contacts or photos

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Track your progress and milestones
  • Calculate health improvements and money saved
  • Provide personalized encouragement
  • Improve the app experience

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain external servers that store your personal quit journey data. If you delete the app, your data is deleted.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For processing subscriptions and payments
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and analytics (anonymized)

7. Children's Privacy

QuitVaping is intended for users 17 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Request information about data we hold
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@quitvaping.app
